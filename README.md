# Privacy Policy — 啾酱笔记 (JapanNote)

**Last updated: May 1, 2026**

---

## Overview

啾酱笔记 is a Japanese vocabulary learning app. We take privacy seriously and have designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

---

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

All data you enter in the app — vocabulary, readings, meanings, notes, and tags — is stored:

- **Locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework
- **In your personal iCloud account** (if iCloud sync is enabled), via Apple's CloudKit

We never have access to your data. It goes directly between your device and your own iCloud account.

---

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your notes are stored in your private iCloud container. This data is:

- Owned entirely by you
- Governed by [Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- Never accessible to us

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings, or delete your iCloud data from:

> **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage**

---

## Home Screen Widget

The widget displays your review count, total notes, and streak. This data is read from your device's local storage via a shared App Group and is never transmitted anywhere.

---

## Third-Party Services

啾酱笔记 uses **no third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services**. There are no SDKs embedded in the app that collect data.

---

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

1. Delete all notes inside the app via **Settings → Delete All Notes**, or
2. Delete the app from your device — all local data is removed automatically
3. To remove iCloud data:
   > **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → 啾酱笔记 → Delete Data from iCloud**

---

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the **Last updated** date above and note the change in the App Store release notes.
